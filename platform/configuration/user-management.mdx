As a workspace admin, you control the full lifecycle of team membership from a single location — **Settings → Users**. This page walks you through inviting new members, deactivating accounts when someone leaves, and importing a large team all at once using a CSV file.

<Info>
  The workspace-level role you assign during invitation sets a default. After a member accepts, you can override their role on any individual project without changing their workspace-level access. See [Roles & Permissions](/platform/administration/roles-permissions) for details.
</Info>

## Invite Members by Email

Inviting members by email is the fastest way to bring individuals or small groups into your workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invite panel">
    Navigate to **Settings → Users → Invite members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email addresses">
    Type one or more email addresses in the input field, separated by commas. You can paste a comma-separated list directly from a spreadsheet or contact list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a workspace role">
    Choose the role each invited member should receive: **Admin**, **Member**, or **Viewer**. If you are inviting a mixed group with different roles, send separate invitations for each role level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitations">
    Click **Send invitations**. Each address receives an email with a unique sign-up link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Invitation links expire after **72 hours**. If a member misses the window, find their entry in the **Pending invites** list under Settings → Users and click **Resend** to issue a fresh link.
</Note>

## Deactivate Accounts

When a team member leaves your organization, deactivating their account immediately revokes their access to the workspace without deleting any of their data or content. Every item they created — projects, comments, files, and settings they configured — remains intact and fully accessible to other members.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the member">
    Navigate to **Settings → Users** and find the member you want to deactivate. Use the search bar to filter by name or email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the member menu">
    Click the **⋯** (more options) button on the member's row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deactivate the account">
    Select **Deactivate account** and confirm the action in the dialog that appears.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The member's status changes to **Deactivated** immediately and their active sessions are invalidated. They will not be able to log in until the account is reactivated.

<Tip>
  To reactivate a deactivated account, return to **Settings → Users**, filter by **Deactivated**, click **⋯** on the member's row, and select **Reactivate**. Their original workspace role and all project memberships are fully restored — no need to reconfigure anything.
</Tip>

## Bulk CSV Import

When you are onboarding a larger team at once, uploading a CSV file is faster than sending individual invitations. Microset reads the file and dispatches invitations to every valid address it finds.

Download the CSV template from the **Settings → Users** page by clicking **Import via CSV → Download template** to ensure your file uses the correct column names and format.

### Required and optional columns

| Column | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `email` | ✅ Yes | The member's work email address. Must be unique within the workspace. |
| `role` | ✅ Yes | Workspace role to assign: `admin`, `member`, or `viewer` (case-insensitive). |
| `first_name` | No | Pre-fills the member's display name on their profile. |
| `last_name` | No | Pre-fills the member's last name on their profile. |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your CSV file">
    Open the downloaded template and populate each row with the member's details. Save the file in CSV format (UTF-8 encoding recommended for names with special characters).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the file">
    Navigate to **Settings → Users → Import via CSV** and click **Choose file**, then select your prepared CSV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the preview">
    Microset displays a preview table showing how it will parse each row. Rows with formatting errors are highlighted — correct those in your file and re-upload if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the import">
    Click **Import members**. Invitations are sent immediately to every valid email address in the file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  CSV import sends invitations **immediately** when you click **Import members**. There is no draft or scheduled import mode. Review the preview table carefully before confirming, especially if you are importing hundreds of addresses at once.
</Warning>

## Security posture

Microset is a non-custodial Solana prediction market. The security goals are:
(1) users sign every on-chain action; (2) market reserves move only by fixed
on-chain rules from program-owned vault PDAs; (3) outcome-token supply is fixed
(mint authority revoked); and (4) administrative powers are limited, two-step, and
disclosed.

## Smart contract risks reviewed

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Access control">
    Single global `Config` authority with two-step transfer. Whitelist and
    resolution paths are authority/council gated. [TODO: confirm the authority key
    is a multisig.]
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fund custody & payouts">
    Reserves are held in a program-owned vault PDA and paid out by the AMM and
    `claim_winnings` formulas; native-SOL payouts preserve rent-exemption. No path
    lets an operator transfer arbitrary user funds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Token safety">
    YES/NO mints have their mint authority revoked to `None` after the initial
    supply, fixing supply. Quote assets are restricted to a whitelisted set.
  </Accordion>

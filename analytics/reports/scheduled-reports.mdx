---
title: "Automate Report Scheduling and Delivery in Microset"
sidebarTitle: "Scheduled Reports"
description: "Automate report delivery on a daily, weekly, or monthly cadence and send results directly to team inboxes or Slack channels without manual effort."
---

Scheduled reports let Microset do the work of running and distributing your most important reports on a recurring cadence. Once you configure a schedule, Microset runs the report automatically and delivers the results — to email inboxes, Slack channels, or both — without any manual action from you or your team.

## Create a schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a saved report">
    Navigate to **Analytics → Reports** and open the report you want to schedule. Schedules can only be created for saved reports; if your report is still unsaved, save it first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Schedule delivery">
    Click the **Schedule delivery** button in the report toolbar. The schedule configuration panel opens on the right side of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the frequency">
    Choose how often you want the report to run:

    | Frequency | Delivers |
    |---|---|
    | **Daily** | Once every day at the time you choose |
    | **Weekly** | Once per week on the day and time you choose |
    | **Monthly** | Once per month on the date and time you choose |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the day and time">
    Select the specific day (for weekly or monthly schedules) and the time of delivery. All times use your **workspace timezone**, which is visible below the time picker. To change your workspace timezone, go to **Settings → Workspace → General**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure recipients">
    Click **Next** to move to the recipients step, where you choose where to deliver the report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choose recipients

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email">
    Enter one or more email addresses in the **Recipients** field, pressing Enter or comma after each address to add it.

    - Recipients **do not** need to be Microset workspace members — you can deliver reports to any valid email address, including external stakeholders and clients.
    - Each email delivery includes:
      - A **summary header** with the report name, run time, and row count
      - An **inline preview** of the first 10 rows of data
      - A **link to the full report** in Microset (requires a workspace account to view)
      - An optional **attached CSV** of the complete dataset — toggle this on in the delivery options step

    <Tip>
      Use the attached CSV option when recipients need to work with the raw data locally or share it in tools outside Microset.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    Connect your Slack workspace to Microset if you have not already done so, then select a **channel** or a **direct message recipient** from the dropdown.

    - Microset posts a **summary card** to the selected destination that includes the report name, row count, and key metrics surfaced from the report.
    - The card includes a **link to the full report** in Microset for anyone who wants to drill deeper.
    - You can configure both a Slack destination and email recipients on the same schedule — Microset delivers to all of them simultaneously.

    <Info>
      Slack delivery requires the Slack integration to be connected for your workspace. For instructions on connecting Slack and other integrations, see the [Integrations quickstart](/integrations/quickstart).
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

After configuring recipients, click **Save schedule** to activate delivery. Microset confirms the schedule and shows the next scheduled run time.

## Manage schedules

To review and manage all scheduled reports in your workspace, go to **Analytics → Reports → Schedules**. Every active and paused schedule is listed there with its frequency, next run time, and recipient count.

From the Schedules page you can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pause a schedule" icon="pause">
    Temporarily stop a schedule without deleting it. Microset retains all settings so you can resume delivery at any time with a single click.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit a schedule" icon="pencil">
    Modify the frequency, delivery time, or recipient list for any existing schedule. Changes take effect starting with the next scheduled run.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Run now" icon="play">
    Trigger an immediate delivery outside the regular cadence — useful for ad-hoc sharing or confirming that a new schedule is working correctly before the first automatic run.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete a schedule" icon="trash">
    Permanently remove the schedule. Deleting a schedule does not delete the underlying report. This action cannot be undone.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Email recipients can opt out of a specific scheduled report at any time by clicking the **Unsubscribe** link in the email footer. Admins can also remove individual recipients directly from the schedule's settings on the Schedules management page.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Deleting a report that has an active schedule automatically removes the schedule as well. Download or duplicate the report before deleting if you want to preserve the delivery configuration.
</Warning>
